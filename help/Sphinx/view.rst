View
=====

.. list-table::
   :width: 100%
   :widths: 30 70
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Shortcut
     - Description
   * - LMB drag + Shift (left)
     - move (pan, keep screen plane)
   * - LMB drag + Shift (right) 
     - move (glide, keep camera altitude)      
   * - LMB drag + Ctrl  
     - rotate (pivot, keep camera position) 
   * - LMB drag + Ctrl + Shift 
     - rotate (pivot with anchor) 
   * - LMB drag + Alt   
     - rotate (orbit, keep camera target)       	 
   * - LMB drag + Alt + Shift   
     - rotate (orbit with anchor)  
	 
	 
.. list-table::
   :width: 100%
   :widths: 30 70
   
   * - MW   
     - zoom (around cursor)
   * - MW + Shift   
     - recenter 	 
   * - MW + Ctrl   
     - zoom (around screen center)
   * - MW + Alt   
     - zoom (around screen center)	 
	 
	 
.. list-table::
   :width: 100%
   :widths: 30 70
   
   * - Ctrl + Tab
